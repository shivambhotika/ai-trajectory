AI in one sentence: Teaching computers to find patterns in data so they can make predictions or decisions.

How it works:

  1. Show the computer millions of examples (books, images, conversations)
  2. It finds patterns ("cats have pointy ears", "this word usually follows that word")
  3. When you ask it something new, it uses those patterns to respond

Why it needs so much power: Finding patterns in billions of examples requires trillions of calculations. More examples + more calculations = better AI.
